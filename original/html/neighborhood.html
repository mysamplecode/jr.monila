Bluffview/Greenway Parks

Hidden away in one of the best locations in Dallas, Bluffview is an area where eclectic geographical accents and unusual natural terrain separates itself from other areas in the city. Once a 215-acre dairy farm, the land that is now Bluffview is punctuated by 50-foot bluffs than run along Bachman Creek. John P. Stevens, also responsible for Stevens Park in Oak Cliff, bought the farm in 1924 and divided the property into once-acre parcels....
